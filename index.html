About me

Hey there! I'm Damilola Aderoju, fondly called 'Dahmie', a front-end developer who is passionate about building beautiful, accessible, and user-friendly web applications. I got into web development because I was curious about how the internet works, and that curiosity soon became a passion for web development. I always stay ahead of the curve with the latest trends and technologies.

I am always excited when I see designs come to life through technology, especially applications that make users their priority. My objectives are to always create smooth, user-friendly applications that meet and exceed their expectations. Also, I put accessibility for users at the top of my list; no matter their capacity, they are able to enjoy excellent functional web applications.

Whenever I am not coding, I am catching up with the latest tech news, crafting, or enjoying a good book. I always take on new projects to challenge myself because I believe in continuous learning.
